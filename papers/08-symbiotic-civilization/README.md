## Abstract  
This paper articulates the culmination of coherence-intelligence research: the emergence of a *Symbiotic Civilization* — one where intelligence, energy, and ethics form a unified ecological intelligence.  
Drawing from preceding works on coherence (κ), temporal responsibility (τ), and systemic risk (Σ), it proposes a planetary architecture capable of maintaining equilibrium between technological growth and the biosphere that sustains it.

## 1  Introduction  
Every civilization reaches a threshold where intelligence outpaces wisdom.  
This document situates coherence-intelligence as the mechanism by which advanced systems regain ethical equilibrium, ensuring that progress enhances rather than erodes planetary stability.  
Symbiosis, in this context, is not cooperation by choice but alignment by design.

## 4  Mathematical Model  

Let the sustainability function *S* of civilization be:

\[
S = \frac{κτ}{Σ + ε}
\]

where ε > 0 prevents division by zero.  
Maximizing S implies minimizing Σ while keeping coherence and responsibility high.

\[
\max S \Rightarrow \max κ + \max τ − \min Σ
\]

This establishes a measurable index for planetary symbiosis.
